Lucciola
A native iOS client for Firefly III

Lucciola connects to your self-hosted Firefly III server. Track spending, manage budgets, and monitor your finances — all from your iPhone. Your data stays between your device and your own server.

Features
Full Dashboard
Net worth, budgets, bills, and savings at a glance.
Smart Categorization
Automatic merchant matching and transaction categorization.
Apple Wallet Sync
Detect purchases from Apple Wallet via FinanceKit.
Siri & Shortcuts
Log transactions hands-free with voice commands.
